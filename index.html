pebbles Found In The Beach


Pebbles are small, smooth stones that are found on the beaches.
They are formed when larger rocks are worn down by the constant pounding of waves, water, or wind.
Pebbles are often rounded or oval in shape. Pebbles are typically smooth to the touch, with a glossy or matte finish.
Pebbles can be found in a wide range of colors, including gray, brown, white, black, and various shades of red, orange, and yellow.
